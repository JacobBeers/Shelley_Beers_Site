About

Shelley Beers is an author, educator, mother, and minister. She is currently working on a series of romantic mistery novels set amongst the rolling hills and winding rivers of the region she calls home - the Ozarks.

Book Title

Shelley fell in love with literature as a child, and has always enjoyed reading and writing. Her first mystery novel, "Master" follows the story of a young teacher who finds herself in a web of trouble when she searches for answers to her brother's murder.

Public Speaking

After years of experience leading womens' and youth ministry efforts at Steelville First Assembly of God, as well as educating in the pubic school system, Shelley now enjoys speaking as a guest at churches, siminars, workshops, and bookstores. She has a passion for sharing God's love with her community in an engaging and fun way.
