About

Shelley Beers is an author, educator, mother, and minister. She is currently working on a series of romantic mistery novels set amongst the rolling hills and winding rivers of the region she calls home - the Ozarks.

"Moonlight Masterpiece"

Shelley fell in love with literature as a child, and has always enjoyed reading and writing. Her first mystery novel, "Moonlight Masterpiece" follows the story of a young teacher who finds herself in a web of trouble when she searches for answers to her brother's murder.

Public Speaking

After years of experience leading women’s and youth ministry efforts, as well as educating in the public school system, Shelley now enjoys guest speaking at churches, workshops, and other venues. She weaves captivating stories and relevant truths together, passionately inspiring people to understand and grasp their God-given value. Her engaging and fun messages reveal a need for listeners to grasp who they are, what they’re called to do, and how to accomplish their life’s mission with confidence. She encourages listeners to live abundantly by freeing themselves of bitterness, resolving to be unoffendable, and choosing victorious courage over fear.
